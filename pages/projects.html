Research Projects
  1. Automatic Karyotyping of Human chromosomes: The process of karyotyping is usually carried out manually by experts for diagnosis of birth defects or various disorders. We have automated the step of chromosome classification in the karyotyping using Deep neural networks. Due to scarcity of data, we have implemented Siamese Networks (Paper) which expediate the training and testing process, keeping the accuracy same as that of traditional CNN. Currently, our focus is on Attention based CNN + LSTM model in order to boost the accuracy.

  2. Extracting information from Document Images: In manufacturing units, the inspection of machines is carried out manually by an engineer who marks the fault type and zone using handwritten arrows and text on the standardized inspection sheet. These sheets are then read manually by a team to maintain a log of the faults in their systems, which is quite time consuming. Therefore, we are building a multiple stage pipeline (Paper) to automate this step. Currently, our emphasis is on the last stage i.e., handwritten text recognition and writing this data to log files.

Projects which formed a part of my graduate requirements:

  1. Shadow Removal and automatic segmentation in OCT images of Optic nerve head: Segmentation of human optic nerve head (ONH) in Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) images becomes difficult when shadow appear due to presence of blood vessels. We aim to improve the quality of OCT images by removal of shadows and then automatically segmenting the retinal layer of interest in Spectral domain OCT using graph theory and dynamic programming. After extraction of choroid layer using segmentation and SSIM techniques, we focused our interest to extract the blood vessels present in it.

    2. Tools used: MATLAB

  2. Object detection using Support Vector Machines: Binary SVM classifier was trained for car detection with foreground (containing object) and background (without object) images using UIUC image dataset. HOG features were extracted from images to train this model and Non-Maximum Suppression is applied to get boundary box for detected object.

    3. Tools used: MATLAB
