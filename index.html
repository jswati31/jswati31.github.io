About

My name is Swati. Currently, I am a Researcher at TCS Innovation Labs, Gurgaon and working under the supervision of Dr. Gautam Shroff and Dr. Lovekesh Vig. I am working in the field of Computer vision and Deep learning applied to Medical Imaging and Document analysis. Also, while working in TCS, I got the opportunity to study Deep learning course organised by TCS Innovation Labs in collaboration with various IIT professors.

Before joining TCS, I was a Master Student at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Hyderabad. My master project was focused on design of Language Identification system using unsupervised spectral clustering techniques. I completed my Master's thesis under the supervision of Dr. K Sri Rama Murty.
