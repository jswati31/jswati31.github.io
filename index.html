I am Swati Jindal, a fifth year Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Cruz, working with Prof. Roberto Manduchi. My research interests focus on the intersection of computer vision and deep learning, with a particular focus on image- and video-based gaze tracking. I was fortunate to have the opportunity to collaborate with Prof. Xin Eric Wang.

I was a Researcher at TCS Innovation Labs, India working with Dr. Gautam Shroff and Dr. Lovekesh Vig. I completed my Master's degree from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Hyderabad working with Prof. K Sri Rama Murty.
